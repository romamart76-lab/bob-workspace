Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

How is this different from free formatters?

Free tools are often buggy, lack mobile preview, and have no support. We built a premium experience: reliable formatting, live preview (desktop + mobile), 20 templates, and responsive support. Think of it as the difference between a free text editor and VS Code.

Why not just use a $30/month platform?

Most $20-30/month tools include scheduling, AI content generation, analytics, and team features. If you just want formatting, you're paying for bloat. We focus on doing ONE thing exceptionally well: formatting. Premium quality, without the enterprise price tag.

Does this work on mobile?

Currently Chrome extension only (desktop). However, our live preview shows you exactly how your post looks on mobile LinkedIn, so you can format on desktop and know it'll look perfect on mobile. Mobile extension coming in Q2 2026.

What if LinkedIn adds native formatting?

LinkedIn has had 8 years and 1 billion users to add formatting. They haven't. If they do, we'll celebrate (and likely sunset the tool or expand to other platforms like X, Threads, Facebook). Until then, we're solving a real problem for 100K+ creators.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel with one click from your account dashboard. No questions asked. No hidden fees. If you cancel mid-month, you keep access until the end of your billing period.

Do you store my LinkedIn posts?

No. All formatting happens locally in your browser. We don't store, read, or access your LinkedIn content. Zero data collection. Privacy-first by design.

What's included in the templates?

20 curated post structures: hook templates (pattern interrupt, curiosity gap), story templates (hero's journey, before/after), list templates (lessons learned, mistakes), and CTA templates (engagement, lead gen). More templates added monthly based on user requests.

Is there a student or creator discount?

Email us at support@linkedinformatter.com with proof of student status or portfolio link (for creators with <1K followers). We offer 50% off for verified students and early-stage creators.
